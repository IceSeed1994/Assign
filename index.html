Trip to Assy Plateau

The main feature of the natural location is the Assy-Turgen astrophysical observatory (2750 meters), which was built in 1975. The structure was built here due to the ideal astroclimate and the absence of light pollution.

Charyn Canyon

This is a picturesque natural area that amazes with its unearthly landscapes. The depth of the canyon is 200 m. The height of the steep mountains of the canyon reaches 150-300 m. The canyon itself is a natural monument, composed of sedimentary rocks, whose age reaches 12 million years. The landscapes here are truly amazing. At sunset, when the sun goes below the horizon, the Charyn canyon turns scarlet, pink and orange. There is silence all around, and only from below you can hear how the stormy streams of the Charyn River beat against the sheer cliffs. This spectacle will not leave anyone indifferent.
