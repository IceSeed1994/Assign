Trip to Kaiyndy Lake

On the territory of Kainda there are several popular locations that you can visit. These include the observation deck "Panorama" where a picturesque view of the expanse of water opens, as well as the "Gate of Desires".

Trip to Assy Plateau

The main feature of the natural location is the Assy-Turgen astrophysical observatory (2750 meters), which was built in 1975. The structure was built here due to the ideal astroclimate and the absence of light pollution.

Charyn Canyon

This is a picturesque natural area that amazes with its unearthly landscapes. The depth of the canyon is 200 m. The height of the steep mountains of the canyon reaches 150-300 m. The canyon itself is a natural monument, composed of sedimentary rocks, whose age reaches 12 million years. The landscapes here are truly amazing.
